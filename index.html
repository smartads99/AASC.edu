OUR EIGHT ABILITIES IN ACTION

Alverno's approach to learning centers around 8 Abilities that strengthen skills in communication, analysis, problem solving, valuing in decision making, social interaction, developing a global perspective, effective citizenship, and aesthetic engagement.

The college's annual Community Day provides wide opportunities to put these abilities into action. Learn more about the 2022 event in coverage provided by TMJ4 in Milwaukee.

DIVERSITY, EQUITY & INCLUSION

At Alverno, we embrace diversity, equity and inclusion (DE&I) and invite you to learn more about the ongoing work as we strive to achieve inclusive excellence.

We are dedicated to student success and to ensuring a positive experience for students and their families, from the beginning of the college search through graduation.
